Our story

We've been Making Delicious Foods Since 1995

Cuisine Restaurant is a free-spirited, modern bistro providing an escape in the Northeast Arts district of Minneapolis. Cuisine’s inspiration is found in the Four Corners region of France and northern Italy.

Go on an adventurous culinary roadtrip with offerings of twists on Italian and French standards, like shrimp ceviche, robust oatmeal, our selections of avocados and toasts, and the seven different types of pies with your choice of juicy fruits, graham crust, flour or buckwheat bread.
